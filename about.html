We don't take on freelancers. We build a roster. Each lead has been on a Fuelled brief before they run one. The roster is capped at six leads so producers don't change mid-engagement. You meet the people who'll do the work before you sign.

Five principles, no slogans

Commercial. Strategic. Central. Transparent. Vetted.

Anchored in behaviour, not slogans.

  1. 01

    Commercial-first.

    We won't shoot if we shouldn't. Strategy starts with the commercial outcome, not the creative idea. We've talked clients out of bigger retainers than the one we ended up signing.

  2. 02

    Strategy-led.

    No camera comes out before the strategy is signed off. If the strategy can't be measured against a P&L line, we don't ship the strategy.

  3. 03

    Centrally directed.

    Steve and Lisa own the through-line on every retainer. The roster executes to brief. Your producer doesn't change mid-engagement.

  4. 04

    Transparent by design.

    Pricing on the site. Notice terms on the site. Asset clauses on the site. No surprise media fees. No "it depends" answers.

  5. 05

    Vetted and capped.

    Roster builds slowly. Capped at six leads. Continuity is structural, not personal. You meet the people who'll do the work before you sign.
