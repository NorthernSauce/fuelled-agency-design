Quick answers

The questions we get on the way in.

If you've got a sixth, ask on the call. We tell you straight.

Is the first call really free?

Yes. No fee, no proposal, no obligation. Thirty minutes on the phone, founder to founder. If we're a fit, we'll book the next conversation. If we're not, we'll tell you on the call and point you somewhere honest.

Will I get a hard sell?

No. We turn down more enquiries than we take. The call is a fit-test for both sides, and the only follow-up is the one you ask for. If we don't hear from you, you don't hear from us.

What should I have ready?

Just the basics. The website. A rough sense of what you're trying to move in the next quarter or two. The audience you actually want messaging you. We do the rest.

Will you tell me the price on the call?

If we're a fit, yes. We don't list packages because every shoot day is different, but the number gets to you on the call. From £475 a month, all in. No surprise media fees, no 60-day notice clauses, no "it depends" answers.

What if I'm not ready to start yet?

That's fine. Some of our best clients booked the call months before they were ready. We'd rather meet you early and properly than chase a closing. The conversation is useful either way.
