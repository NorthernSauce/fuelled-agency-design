Why your last three engagements didn't land

Three models. None of them work for marketing teams at £5m+ brands.

01 Volume-retainer agencies

Sold by the post. Priced by the brand spend they can't influence.

The deliverables-retainer model rewards volume over value. Content gets pushed to junior staff juggling six other clients. The senior account director who pitched you doesn't run your account, and the work shows it.

02 In-house hires

Six months to ramp. Twelve months until they leave.

Two juniors cost £52k base. Plus NI, pension, kit, training, sick days, day-one rights. A four-person in-house team is £190k to £250k loaded. They can post and caption. They can't direct a shoot or run a multi-market strategy.

03 Freelancer roster

Brilliant briefs. Until one of them goes on holiday.

Cheap freelancers caption and schedule. Some are brilliant; most ghost when something better comes along. None of them carries the strategy across the team. The brief drift is what kills the through-line.

The Five-Step Plug-In

One team. One brief. One bill. Five steps that run as your team would.

Strategy · Shoot · Edit · Post · Improve

The Five-Step Plug-In is how we run Fuelled. It's how we kill Tactical Drift. Centrally directed, roster-delivered, briefed once.

  1. 01

    Strategy. Commercial outcome agreed first.

    Lisa runs the strategy phase on every retainer. Before any camera comes out, we agree the commercial outcome, the audience, the measurement plan, and the channels. The strategy doc is yours from day one.

  2. 02

    Shoot. Lean crew, full kit.

    Our team directs, or one of the named roster leads who's been on a Fuelled brief before. Cameras, lighting, shot list, sound. The brand strategy decides what we shoot. We don't busk it.

  3. 03

    Edit. In-house suite. Approval-routed.

    Final Cut Pro, Premiere, CapCut, retouching. Every asset cut to its destination platform, colour-graded, captioned. Routed for your approval before publish. Nothing ships without you signing off.

  4. 04

    Post. Paid social where it earns.

    Scheduled across the platforms that matter. Paid social layered on top where the strategy calls for it. Paid social only - no Google Ads (priced past the point of attribution in our verticals).

  5. 05

    Improve. Analyse, improve, plan and report.

    Monthly review against the strategy. Attribution disclosed (date range, organic vs paid, view-through tracked). What worked. What didn't. What we're changing next month. The deck you take upstairs.

How we work

Commercial. Strategy-led. Central. Transparent. Hand-picked.

Five principles, anchored in behaviour. No slogans.

  1. 01

    Commercial-first.

    We won't shoot if we shouldn't. Strategy starts with the commercial outcome, not the creative idea. We've talked clients out of bigger retainers than the one we ended up signing.

  2. 02

    Strategy-led.

    We own the strategy and run it. It's not a deliverable we hand over and walk away from. No camera comes out before it's signed, and it stays live on the retainer.

  3. 03

    Centrally directed.

    The through-line is owned on every retainer. The roster executes. Your producer doesn't change mid-engagement.

  4. 04

    Transparent by design.

    Pricing on the site. Notice terms on the site. Asset clauses on the site. No surprise media fees. No "it depends" answers on the first call.

  5. 05

    Hand-picked.

    Small, hand-picked roster. The producer who starts your retainer is the one who finishes it.
