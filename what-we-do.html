How a month runs

Five steps. One team. No handoffs.

  1. 01

    Strategy.

    Before any camera comes out, we sit down. We map the audience, the angles, and the sectors you actually want messaging you. The output is a one-page document that drives every shoot day after.

  2. 02

    Shoot.

    One structured day a month. We turn up with the crew, the lighting, the kit. You turn up as you. We direct the rest. Six to eight hours, depending on scale.

  3. 03

    Edit.

    We cut the day into a month of content. Reels, posts, stories, long-form. All shot in one go, not chased week to week. You see the full set before anything is scheduled.

  4. 04

    Post.

    We schedule it. We caption it. We hashtag it where it still helps. Nothing posts without your sign-off. The point of the shoot day is that you don't think about your social again until next month.

  5. 05

    Improve.

    End of every month, we look at what worked. The next shoot leans into it. Nothing is set in stone, the strategy doc gets updated, and your producer sends you a one-page summary. No 30-slide deck.

Straight answers

The questions everyone asks first.

Will it take hours of my time to make this content?

No. The shoot day is the time investment. Six to eight hours, once a month. After that, your only job is approving the posts before they go live. Most clients spend less than half an hour a week on this once we're up and running.

Can I keep the assets if we stop working together?

Yes. Every file we shoot is yours. The raws, the edits, the strategy document. You walk with all of it. We don't believe in holding assets hostage. Your business funded the shoot, your business owns the output.

Can I approve every post before it goes live?

Yes. Nothing posts without your sign-off. We send the month's plan, you tick what you like, we adjust the rest. After the first month or two, most clients let us run with light-touch approval, but it's your call.

What's the notice period?

One month. No 60-day clauses. No annual retainer lock-in. If it isn't working, you stop. We'd rather lose a client than trap one. The honest version: most agencies use long notice periods to mask churn. We don't need to.

What if my business isn't a good fit?

We'll tell you on the call. We turn down more enquiries than we take. If content isn't going to move the needle for you, we'll say so, and point you somewhere that will. The first call costs you nothing and there's no proposal-by-stealth.

Do you do paid ads?

No. We're the production and posting partner. If you're running ads alongside us, our content gives you better material to spend on. Most clients see the organic work outperform paid once the content is genuinely good.

Where do you shoot?

UK and Europe. Most days are in the UK. Some are in Mallorca and the wider Mediterranean for hospitality and yachting clients. Travel is built into the number on the call, no surprise invoices.

Thirty minutes on the phone. We'll tell you straight whether content will move the needle for you, before you've spent a penny. No proposal-by-stealth. No hard sell. If it isn't the right fit, we'll say so.
