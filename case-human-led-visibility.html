The brief

Stop chasing volume. Start being findable to the right person.

This wasn't a follower-count play. The client wanted enquiries from non-EU nationals who could afford the service and were close to making a move. The content had to feel human, on camera, and answer the questions that usually get whispered to a friend over WhatsApp.

Tax. Residency. Schools. NIE numbers. The unglamorous, decision-stage stuff. The kind of detail that earns trust before a single sales call happens.

  • Real audience map
  • Founder on camera
  • Search-led titles
  • Share-friendly formats
Reels that landed

Founder on camera. Plain English answers.

How it ran

Three moves. Right audience, right country, right intent.

01

Map the real audience.

Not just "expats". UK, US, German and Swedish nationals making a permanent move, with a budget and a deadline. The brief got specific before the camera moved.

  • Country-specific buyer questions
  • Move-stage by month and year
  • Budget bands and decision triggers
02

Put the founder on camera.

All the awkward questions, answered by the person who actually does the work. Tax. Residency. Schools. The stuff that earns trust because it's specific.

  • One question per video
  • Founder-led, no script readers
  • Captions optimised for silent watch
03

Target at the content layer.

No ads. Targeting happened in the writing, the titling, and the hashtag use. The right country, the right query, the right answer.

  • Search-led titles
  • Country-tagged content
  • Share-friendly formats so it travels in WhatsApp groups

A 128 percent click lift, no spend, and growth landed exactly where the client wanted it. Quality over volume, every time.
