The brief

Premium yachting buyers don't react to ads.

The luxury yachting buyer reacts to texture, craft, and the people behind the brand. Aggressive paid placements feel like noise to them. The brief was to build documentary-style content with the depth to carry a long sales cycle. Show, don't shout.

One shoot day. One narrative edit. Then short cuts pulled from the same day for socials. Same story, sized for the channel.

  • Texture over polish
  • People over product
  • Long form for YouTube
  • Short cuts for social

How it ran

Three moves. One shoot. A library of work.

01

Find the story.

A day with the people who actually run the brand. Founders, captains, makers, on camera. The story comes from them, not from a script.

  • Pre-shoot conversations to draw out the real angle
  • A loose shot list, not a locked storyboard
  • Room for the day to surprise us
02

Cut for craft.

Edit pace, sound design, colour. Documentary, not advertising. The viewer should feel the place before they read a word of caption.

  • Long-form narrative cut for YouTube
  • Vertical short cuts pulled from the same edit
  • Sound-led, captions optional
03

Place it where the buyer is.

Long-form on YouTube. Short cuts on Reels and TikTok. The same story, sized for the channel and the moment.

  • YouTube for depth and search
  • Reels and TikTok for first impression
  • Stills for the website and brokerage decks

Ten days. Eighteen thousand views. Eighty-eight new followers. Zero spend.
