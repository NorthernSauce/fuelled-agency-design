The brief

Content starts before the camera turns on.

The most valuable content is shaped long before the lens opens. The client didn't want a few photos and a couple of videos. They needed content that could carry the website, drive product sales, fill the events calendar, and tell the founder's story. One brand, four very different jobs.

Instead of four separate shoots, we designed a single day that would tell the whole story. Every scene engineered to produce multiple usable assets across multiple channels.

  • Website visuals
  • Product-led sales content
  • Event promotion
  • Personal brand storytelling
On location Mallorca · artisan tasting · lifestyle
How it ran

Three moves. One day. The whole story.

01

The mood board.

Every project starts here. Not a Pinterest scrape. A creative-direction document that tells everyone in the room what we're making and how it should feel.

  • Atmosphere the brand should carry
  • Textures, colours and environments that feel real
  • Emotional tone of the work
  • The balance between product, people, and place

The aim was natural, grounded, experience-led. Visuals that read like the brand actually exists, not visuals that read like an ad.

02

Stories, not shots.

We don't plan isolated images. We map scenes. Each scene engineered to generate multiple layers of content, all from one moment.

  • Product content — website + product pages
  • Environmental shots — atmosphere + lifestyle
  • Event visuals — marketing for experiences
  • Personal brand — humanising the founder

One moment, captured well, becomes a reel, a still, a sales-page hero, and a founder portrait. That's how a hundred assets come from a single day.

03

Captured with intent.

On the day, we work the planned scenes. Then we leave room for what the day actually wants to do.

  • Real conversations
  • Natural moments
  • Movement and atmosphere

Those unscripted seconds usually make the strongest content. They show the brand as it actually exists. Not a staged advertisement. The thing itself.
