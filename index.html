The Five-Step Plug-In

Five steps. One team. No handoffs.

One strategy. One brief. One bill.

The freelancer model breaks the minute somebody goes on holiday. The agency model bills by deliverable. The in-house model can't be staffed at premium production quality without burning your headcount budget. We replace the lot with one team you brief once.

  1. 01

    Strategy that names the revenue, not the reach.

    Before any camera comes out, we agree the commercial outcome, the audience, and the measurement. The strategy doc is yours from day one.

  2. 02

    Shoot that's directed, not vibed.

    Lean crew, full kit, agreed shot list. Our team directs, or one of the roster leads who knows the brief inside out.

  3. 03

    Edit cut to the outcome.

    In-house edit suite. Final Cut, Premiere, CapCut, retouching. Every asset cut to its destination platform and approval-routed before publish.

  4. 04

    Post on cadence. Not at midnight.

    Scheduled across the platforms that matter. Captions, hashtags, swipe copy. Paid layered on top where the strategy calls for it. You see every post before it ships.

  5. 05

    Improve analyse, improve, plan and report.

    Monthly review against the strategy. Attribution disclosed. What worked. What didn't. What we're changing next month. The deck you take upstairs.
