Questions you'll ask anyway

Five questions every Marketing Director asks before signing.

If you've got a sixth, ask on the call. We tell you straight.

How long until we see attribution?

First measurement report at month three. Baseline established by month six. Monthly attribution reports from there on, with date range, organic vs paid disclosure, and view-through tracking method named on each report.

What happens if Steve or Lisa leaves the engagement?

Both Steve and Lisa are on every retainer. Roster leads are named in your contract. Continuity is structural, not personal - the strategy doc, the brand voice rules, the approval system don't reset if a person rotates off.

Can we cancel after month one?

Yes. One month's notice. You keep the strategy doc, the asset bank, your accounts. No 60-day clause unless you've signed a 12-month retainer for the cadence discount.

Do you do Google Ads?

No. Paid social only. Google Ads in our verticals (hospitality, yachting, lifestyle, aviation) are priced past the point of attribution - the CPC inflation means we can't honestly tie a measurement story back. Paid social, paid placement on the platforms that actually move bookings - yes.

What if we're not sure which tier fits?

Book the 30-minute call. We tell you straight which tier fits, or whether content isn't the right answer for your business right now. We've talked clients out of bigger retainers than the one we ended up signing - we'd rather lose the sale than land you in the wrong tier.
